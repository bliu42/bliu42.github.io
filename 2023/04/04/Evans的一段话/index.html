分享 - Bill Evans的一段话

发表于 2023-04-04 更新于 2023-10-13 阅读次数：

学习爵士乐有感，最近大半年我对物理还是有些浮躁了，科研之路还需较真啊。

I see this in a lot of people that come to me, that for instance for advice or should they go ahead or so on.
很多人来找我寻求比如说建议或是说他们还要不要继续之类的。

They tend to approximate the product, rather than attacking it in a realistic, true way at any elementary level- regardless how elementary, but it must be entirely true, entirely real and entirely accurate.
他们（都有的问题是）倾向于去模仿一个音乐作品，而不是真刀真枪地，在最基础的层面上去对待它。无论多么基础的层面，都必须做到完全正确，完全真实，完全精确。

They would rather approximate the entire problem than to take a small part of it and be real and true about it.
而他们比起取出一小部分认真对待，更倾向于去含糊地将问题大而化之。

I think this is a very important thing that you must be satisfied to be very clear and very real, and to be very analytical at any level.
我认为要做到非常清晰非常实在，且在每一个层面都分析清楚是非常重要的。

You can’t take the whole thing and to approximate it in a vague way, gives one a feeling hat they’ve probably more or less touched this thing.
你不能拿来整个问题然后模糊地近似它，从而给人一种你已经多少掌握了这个东西的错觉。

In this way, you just lead yourself twards confusion. And ultimately you’re going to get so confused that you’ll never find you way out.
这样做只能让你自己迷茫。最终你将会迷茫到根本找不到出路。

And it’s true of any subject, that the person that succeeds in anything, has the realistic viewpoint at the beginning, and knowing that the problem is large, and he has to take it a step at a time, and he has to enjoy the step-by-step learning procedure.
这对于所有学科都是一样的。无论做什么，能取得成功的人从一开始就有着实际的着眼点，知道他面对的问题很复杂，他必须一步一个脚印地去做，并且他一定要享受这个一步一步的学习过程。
